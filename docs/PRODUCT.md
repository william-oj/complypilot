# Product overview

ComplyPilot is a compliance automation platform that guides organizations from setup to ISO 27001 audit readiness.

## Differentiators
- Guided journey wizard
- Plain language workflows
- Automated policies and evidence mapping
- AI assistant for non-experts

## Business model
- Monthly SaaS subscriptions (tiered by org size)
- Consultant partner accounts (multi-org)
- Enterprise licensing with SSO and custom SLAs
